02 — Covenant

A Partnership for Life

Not a product. A relationship.

The Promise

Every KREA transducer is accompanied by a signed agreement — a personal document that establishes a mutual commitment between us and you. Not fine print. A handshake.

Built for Your Vehicle. Only.

The entire electromechanical identity of every transducer is calculated for your specific installation. Resonant frequency, compliance, moving mass, force factor — optimized for your acoustic environment.

Lifetime

Not a warranty. A membership. Unconditional, permanent, personal. When improvements become available, card holders access them at cost — materials and labor only, no markup.

The Specialist

Every KREA installation is performed by a certified specialist, with calibration verified by measurement. Performance is guaranteed by measurable, verifiable acoustic results.

Made for You, Personally

Every KREA is a custom instrument, designed and built for one person and one installation. This is not about exclusivity for its own sake — it is about ensuring that what we create truly works where it is meant to live.

A KREA installed without proper calibration cannot deliver what it was designed to deliver. That is why we work closely with certified specialists to ensure every installation meets our shared expectations.

Because each KREA is optimized for a specific installation, transferring it to a different environment means it may not perform as intended. If your plans change, we are here to help — whether that means recalibrating for a new vehicle or finding the right solution together.

Continuous Evolution

Our work does not end when your KREA is installed. We continue to refine our engineering — motor geometry, suspension chemistry, voice coil winding — and these improvements are available to you.

When improvements become available, Lifetime Card holders access them at cost — materials and labor only, no market markup. You ship your transducer to KREA. It returns upgraded. This is what a lifetime relationship looks like.
